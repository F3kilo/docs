## API Categories

### Trading & Execution
- **Trade Management**: Execute buy/sell orders, manage positions
- **Auto Buy**: Set up automated buying strategies
- **Copy Trading**: Follow and replicate successful traders
- **Swap Execution**: Direct token swaps with optimal routing

### Market Data & Analytics
- **Token Analytics**: Snipers, top traders, and token performance
- **Wallet Analytics**: PnL tracking, swap history, and wallet insights
- **Scanner**: Real-time token scanning and filtering
- **Pair Data**: Price feeds, liquidity, and market information

### Social & Community
- **Telegram Integration**: Connect Telegram channels and calls
- **Twitter Integration**: Monitor Twitter accounts for token calls
- **User Lists**: Create and follow curated token lists
- **Marketplace**: Discover and subscribe to trading strategies

### User Management
- **User Profiles**: Account information and settings
- **Commission Tracking**: Monitor earnings and cashback
- **Notifications**: Manage alerts and notifications
- **Asset Management**: Track wallet balances and holdings
